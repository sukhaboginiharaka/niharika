The Blazing Sands

at Andhra Pradesh-Papikondalu

On my boat ride towards papikondalu the weather was so gratifying.The sky broke like an egg into full sunset and the water caught fire.A lady who wore a vivid coloured saree was striding down the hill to hoard some water from the godavari river.That's the moment when i took this snap.

The luminous light eradicates the negativity and gives a fresh start every morning.
